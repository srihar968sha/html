FEEL THE BGM

AMAZING VFX

Nag Ashwin’s Kalki 2898 AD has been one of the most keenly anticipated films of the year, and recently, the trailer of the film upped the expectations. The film features Amitabh Bachchan, Kamal Haasan, Prabhas, Deepika Padukone and Disha Patani in the lead roles, the script of the film ranges from 3102 BC (the beginning of Kali Yuga) to 2898 AD. The film has been made on the budget of Rs 600 crore, and is considered to be one of the most expensive films in the history of Indian cinema. Nag Ashwin has roped in some of the biggest technicians to work on the film especially in the VFX department. One of the big names he was able to bring on board for the film is a Canada based VFX company called The Embassy. They have been part of some of the biggest films in the world like Iron Man, Captain America and Hunger Games.
